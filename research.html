Why is sleep so important?
It regenerates the brain.

During the night sleep, neural connections regenerate and get stroger. This is when memories get created, knowledge revised and when ideas come to us. Good sleep boosts creativity and productivity.

Does sleep influence our mood? Yes, it does!

Lack of distruptions during a night sleep helps to feel better. The less you sleep, the more likely you are to feel grumpy and loose concentration during the day. Good night sleep is a source of energy for all of us.

Regular sleep is important. Try to wake up at the same time every day.

Inroducing regularity to our daily life helps with more effective falling asleep and waking up, hence improved overall sleep quality. Set a alarm at a regular time and start feeling better.
